# My Journey

## Software Developer and IT Architect ar REWE digital (Since October 2011)
- Since October 2011 I am working at [REWE digital](https://www.rewe-digital.com/en), wich is the larget IT service provider of the [REWE Group](https://www.rewe-group.com/en/)
- During this period, the company underwent major organizational changes:
  - It envoved from an clasical siloed organiaztion to a modern functional oriented product organisation with cross-functional teams that develope and operate their systems. 
  - The motto is "You build it, you run it, you fix it" - kind of an evolution of Werner Vogels' "You build it, you run it" (Gray, Jim; Vogels, Werner (2006). „A Conversation with Werner Vogels“. ACM Queue, Vol. 4, No. 4, S. 14–22). 
  - REWE digital separated disciplinary leadership from functional leadership.
  - It opened subsidiaries in several European countries, transforming itself into an international company
- From Oct 2011 to Now 2020 I worked as Software engineer
  - During that time, the I took a long technological journey with the company:
    - I started with writing batch jobs on a IBM Z mainframe and tomcat webserver being hosted on virtual linux machines that had to be configured by writing Jira tickets
    - In 2020, my team wrote spring-boot jobs and angular applications being hosted on Kubernetes clusters. The applicaion were deployed and configured automaticall via sophisticagted CI/CD pipelines
  - Personally, I was stronlg influences by the book [Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship](https://www.thalia.de/shop/home/artikeldetails/A1003067068) by Robert C. Martin (ISBN: 9780132350884)
    - community [Clean Code Developer](https://clean-code-developer.de/en/)
    - test.driven devlopement
- Since Dec 2020 I am working as IT Architect
  - DDD
    - hexagon vs microservice vs modulith vs monolith
  - architecture elevator
  - accelerate


## Research associate at Fraunhofer IAIS (July 2007 - September 2011)
- From July 2007 - September 2011, I worked as research associate at [Fraunhofer IAIS](https://www.iais.fraunhofer.de/)
- Most of my publications are from these days
- My first project was the research initiative [DESIRE](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/DESIRE), where I contributed to a the control engine for the whole system
- My main focus throughout this period was conducting studies for the German Armed Forces.
  - This included simulations that were carried out using data farming methods and subsequently analyzed.
  - In addition, we applied optimization techniques such as genetic algorithms and linear optimization to specific military scenarios.
